About Avon

At Avon, we are committed to empowering women everywhere by offering high-quality beauty products that inspire confidence and individuality. With a history dating back to 1886, Avon has been a global leader in direct sales, giving women the tools to achieve financial independence and success.

We believe in the power of beauty to make the world a better place, and we are proud to offer a diverse range of skincare, makeup, fragrance, and wellness products to suit every need. Our mission is to enrich the lives of our customers and representatives by providing the highest level of service, support, and opportunity.

Our Core Values

Empowerment

We empower women to take control of their lives, both personally and professionally.

Integrity

We are committed to doing the right thing for our customers, representatives, and the communities we serve.

Innovation

We constantly strive to create innovative products and solutions that meet the evolving needs of our customers.
